ADDRESS change

whb@hoh-2.att.com
(maybe, true)

This is an actual letter, recieved by my mother.  In deference to
her, but out of no love for the sender, I've changed all names and
addresses.

-----

The ABCD Company
1234 Main St.
Columbus, Ohio 43215
Member New York Stock Exchange

JANE DOE
123 MAPLE ST.
SMALLTOWN, OHIO 43210

Recently, we were advised of an address change.  Since the U.S. Postal
Service automatically forwards mail we sent this notice to your previous
address.  This assures notification in the event we recorded an incorrect
new address.  If you wish to make any corrections, please note, sign and
return this letter.  There is no need to respond if the new address is
accurate.

		PREVIOUS
		--------

	JANE DOE
	123 MAPLE ST.
	SMALLTOWN, OHIO 43210

		NEW
		---

	Jane Doe
	123 Maple St.
	Smalltown, Ohio 43210


SIGNATURE:_____________________________________________
I verify the change to be accurate except for notations.
