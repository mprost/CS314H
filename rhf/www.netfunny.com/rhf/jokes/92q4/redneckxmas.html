A redneck's 12 days of Christmas

ndd@sunbar.mc.duke.edu (Ned Danieley)
(topical, smirk)

Heard 12/22 on a Greensboro, NC, radio station; attributed to Randy
and the Rednecks.


On the nth day of Christmas, my true love gave to me:

12 pack of Bud,
11 wrasslin' tickets,
tin of Copenhagen,
9 years probation,
8 table dancers,
7 packs of Red Man,
6 cans of Spam,
5 flannel shirts,
4 mud-grip tires,
3 shot-gun shells,
2 hunting dogs,
and some parts to a Mustang GT.
