Highly Racist Watermellon Joke

aha@k.cc.purdue.edu (Gregory J. Stiehl)
Purdue University
(rot13, racist, offense=blacks, offense=poles, non stargate, funny)

A polack is walking down the street with a big bag over his shoulder, when a black guy stops him and asks: "Yo and Sheee, Wha be in the bag maaaan?" And the polack replies, "Watermelons." The black guy asks how many and the polack says: "If you can guess how many are in here, I'll give you both of them." So the black guy says, "Ten?"


Greg Stiehl
(aha@k.cc.purdue.edu)
