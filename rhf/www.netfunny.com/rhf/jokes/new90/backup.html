A Beeper Story

cherylm@lsid.hp.com (Cheryl Marks)
(true, chuckle)

A young boy and his mother were standing in a grocery store behind a large woman who wore a beeper on her belt.

Suddenly, the beeper went off.

The little boy cried, "Careful, Mommy--she's backing up!"

(told to me by Burt B--our resident storyteller)
