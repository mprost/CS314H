A group of my friends and I were road-tripping to Toronto, and we got into a
conversation about the recent vote of Quebec to become an independant nation.
Before we all started laughing and pointing at him, my friend Kevin asked, in
total honesty:

"If Quebec leaves Canada, where are they going to go?"
