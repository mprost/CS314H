What a party!

neilg@hpqtdya.sqf.hp.com (Neil Gall)
(smirk)

At a friend's 21st birthday party recently, my mother introduced
me to another guest.  "This is Peter," she said, "you were at his
fourth birthday party."  I didn't recognise the guy at all, and
it's obvious he was thinking the same thing, as he turned to me
and said, "Oh! That must have been some party - I don't remember
you at all!"
