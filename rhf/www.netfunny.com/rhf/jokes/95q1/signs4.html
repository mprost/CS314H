From: loewenst@spdc.ti.com (Lee Loewenstein)

at the snack bar:

   -------------
  |  ICE CREAM  |
  |   TOILETS   |
   -------------

This could explain those cold toilet seats.  To be safe though, I'd recommend
you get your ice cream in a cup, not a bowl.  And stay away from the chocolate.
