Quote of the Day

monroeb@essex.hsc.colorado.edu (B. Monroe)
(chuckle, computers, academia)

In our Information Systems department, we deal with everyone from Janitore
(oops, sorry, Environmental Services Technicians) to Doctors (just
Doctors, I guess).  Our favorite quote comes from a colleague of mine who
was explaining some facts of the computer world to a physician/instructor
here at the university.  The instructors reply was:

"You can't be telling me that you expect us to buy a new computer EVERY
TEN YEARS??!?"

This person had apparently been running a floppy-based system on an XT,
and it had died.
