Religion is like a complex operating system: it's full of bugs and
the manufacturor keeps making promises about the revised version.

Ever notice how religion and a political campaign are alike? You
can't tell if the promises are true until it's too late to go
back.

Would someone please tell the Great System Operator to put the
goddamn Murphy's Law switch OFF!

The cult books are the technical reference manuals of life wich have been
written by end-users
