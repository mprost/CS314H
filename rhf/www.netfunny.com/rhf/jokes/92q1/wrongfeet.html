True, out of the mouths of babes

herr@jade.ab.ca (Chuck Herr)

(smirk)


A friend told my wife and I this story about her
five year old son, Evan.  Evan is putting his gear
on to go outside:

Mom:  Evan, you've got your boots on the wrong feet.

Evan:  But mom, these are the only feet I have!
