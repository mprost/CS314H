The Family Computer Geek

michael@imagescape.com (Michael Jered Kopp)
(original, smirk, computers)

Recently I attended a family event in Florida. All of my funniest relatives were there, and it was something like a Seinfeld episode come to life.

Tired of explaining to each of my older relatives what being a "web site developer" actually meant, I had started telling people that I was a "computer geek."

"Isn't a geek the person in the circus who bites the heads off of live chickens?" asked one of my wise-cracking relatives.

"I'm a computer geek," I said. "I bite the heads off of America Online Telemarketers."
