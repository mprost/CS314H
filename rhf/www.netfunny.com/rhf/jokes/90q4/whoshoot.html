Sununu Snicker

hforbes@sunne.east.sun.com (harry forbes)
(topical, chuckle)

Question:

You are trapped in a room with: 

1) Mohamar Kadaffi 
2) Saddam Hussein 
3) John Sununu

You have a pistol with only 2 bullets. Who do you shoot?

Answer: Shoot John Sununu. Twice.


A Washington joke circulated by Sununu haters--attributed to
a speech by Mike Dukakis.
