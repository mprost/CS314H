1-800-Make a what???

lsundae@teleport.com (Lady Sundae)
(original, smirk)

My husband and I were watching television last night when the newest
La-z-boy commercial came on.  The ad was stupid as usual right up to the
1-800 number.

They want me to call 1-800-MAKE A HOME.

Excuse me, but, don't they realize that most of America knows that a phone
number is only seven numbers after the 1-800?  They want me to call
1-800-MAKE A HO???
