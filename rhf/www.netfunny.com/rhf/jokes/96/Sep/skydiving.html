Wake-up call

BRODIE@cpg.mcw.edu (Kent C Brodie)
Medical College of Wisconsin Physicians & Clinics
(smirk)

Last weekend, I went skydiving for the very first time.  The class was
well structured, with just enough humor tossed in to keep the class
interesting.      At the tail end of the class, we all took a 31-question
test which we were REQUIRED to pass fully, before stepping into the aircradt.

We all finished the test in about 10 minutes, with the exception of one guy, 
who was apparently pondering one of the questions.   In jest, one of our group
shouted out, "He's stuck on question 14....".

That question read exactly as follows:

14.  During skydiving, parachuting, (or anything else!),

     A. I may be killed or injured.
     B. I may be killed or injured.
     C. I may be killed or injured.
     D. I may be killed or injured.
