Topical Jokes

Topical Jokes

The best thing about an on-line humour magazine is that it arrives twice a day. The latest jokes about news, current events and public figures are always on tap in Rec.Humor.Funny. Enjoy them here.

Many of these jokes have a particular topical context, although you should be able to figure out most of them. They might not be as funny today as they were back when they were published at exactly the right time.

This collection has a special chapter devoted to nothing but Gulf War jokes. By far, most of the topical jokes in 1991 revolved around the war.
