Have I got a deal

alj@mtunb.UUCP
(heard it, chuckle)

Satan comes down to visit a famous, utterly ruthless Hollywood producer. Satan says, "Look, I have a business proposition for you. I can get you any deal you want, with anybody in the business, on any terms you like."

The producer's eyes light up. "Hmm. . . and what do you want from me?"

Satan smiles. "Your immortal soul."

The producer sits back and ponders, stroking his goatee. "I don't get it. Where's the catch?"
