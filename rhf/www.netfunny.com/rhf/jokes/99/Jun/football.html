German Revenge

kht@mactao.demon.nl (MacEddie)
(smirk, sexual, forwarded, offense=English, Germans)

This is a joke about the Euopean soccer game on Wednesday 25 Jun 99.


Why do women love German footballers?

Because they're on top for 90 minutes and still
come second.

The joke was past on a few times and eventually made it to Germany. This was the reply of one of the Germans:


Why are Englismen such lousy lovers?

They'll wait 90 minutes, then put it in twice in
the last minute and think there the worlds best lovers!
