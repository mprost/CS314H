Matrix was unbelievable

bgould@home.com (Brian Jay Gould)
(smirk, computers)

I saw Matrix and thought the thing was totally unbelievable. The part about artificial intelligence taking over the world and humans being used as batteries was OK. The technology was fine, I could believe all of that. I had no problem most of the movie.

But the part when a software development manager insists that a valued programmer show up to work at 9:00AM was just too far fetched for my imagination. That couldn't really happen.... could it?
