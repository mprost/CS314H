We'll miss you, Gene

lcrocker@mercury.colossus.net (Lee Daniel Crocker)
Piclab (external.html/)
(topical, smirk, sick)

Millions of film fans mourn the passing of critic Gene Siskel, who lost his battle with cancer on Sunday at the age of 53.

A special preview of the funeral for the press will be held this Wednesday, followed by services for the general public on Friday.

(I hesitate to make light of good man's death, but Gene himself was fond of sick humor and I think he'd appreciate it.)
