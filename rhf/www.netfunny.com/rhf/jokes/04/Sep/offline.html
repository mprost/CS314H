Email : Please turn on your computer

seandees@ca.inter.net (S. Dees)
(smirk, computers)

I work in a business that has a central dispatch that sends orders through an older computer system that is set up like an older BBS. (Computerized Bulletin Board System). They sent a message today:


>ATTENTION ALL STORES:
>You are offline. Please turn on your computers.

I called them and told them that any store who had their computer turned off would not get this message. They sent a follow-up message for me:


>Store #10 - Is your computer functioning now?

Talk about not being clear on the concept....
