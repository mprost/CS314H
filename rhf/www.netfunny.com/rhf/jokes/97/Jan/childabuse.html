Fanatical Fears

abrams@cs.columbia.edu (Steven Abrams)
(topical, smirk, offense=Jets fans)

Heard on the WNBC-TV (NY) news cast last night.  (As Dave Barry might
say, I am not making this up.)

A local health and fitness club has taken note at the huge number of
no-shows to the NY Jets and Giants home games, of late.  They have
asked ticketholders, rather than to simply not show up, to please
donate their tickets to the club who will then take underprivileged
children to the football games.

A gentleman from Connecticut called the club and asked if they had
insurance.  The answer: "Yes, but why?"  "Well, I'm going to give you
my Jet tickets.  And you're going to take some children to the game.
I just want to make sure I don't get arrested for child abuse."
