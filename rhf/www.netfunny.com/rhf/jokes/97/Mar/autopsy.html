Down to a science....

ksiddiqu@cse.ucsc.edu (Khalil Siddiqui)
(smirk)

In the San Jose Mercury News (2/5/97), it was reported that a young boy
was murdered in Costa Rica for the money in his pocket (about $4).
Despite the tragic nature of the story, the following paragraph killed
me:

"Police first assumed the child had drowned, but an autopsy found he had
been beaten, drowned, and robbed of a ($4 note) he had been carrying."

I've always marvelled at how accurate an autopsy can be, but that's
amazing....
