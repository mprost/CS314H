Down Town Macarana

w4crypto@ix.netcom.com (Walter Gould)

(original, smirk)

A friend of mine, Ethel Cunningham wrote me:

"Have you seen the "Down Town Macarana" (or how ever you spell it).  
Anyway, it's the version they do in downtown LA or Center City Philly
or just about any downtown.  You will have to use some imagination.  

"It's 'right hand up - left hand up, right hand on the wall, left
hand on the wall, head on the wall, right leg out - left leg out,
right hand behind your head, left hand behind your head, right hand
behind our back, left hand behind your back.'  

"All you gotta do is watch "Cops" [on TV] and you can see it."
