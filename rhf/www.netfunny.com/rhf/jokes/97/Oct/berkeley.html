How Berkeley Can You Be

KACKRMAN@aol.com
(smirk, true)

I went to the second annual "How Berkeley Can You Be" parade this morning.
 Before the parade arrived, people dressed as British Bobbies but calling
themselves the "Fashion Police" came along and inspected the waiting
spectators.  Those who passed muster were given a yellow feather.  Those who
did not were given a "ticket" much like a traffic ticket, with a box checked
off for the infraction being cited:

 	Inappropriate dress for body type

 	Failure to yield to good taste

 	Reckless coordinating

 	Excessive accessorizing

 	Dressing to incite parental distress

 	Wardrobe expired

 	Iron not activated

 	Loitering with clothes on

 	Sandal permit not prominently displayed

 	Code 9: Beige load too heavy

 	Bad hair day
