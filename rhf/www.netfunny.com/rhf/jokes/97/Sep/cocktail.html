Four Weddings and a Funeral?

razon@vipe.technion.ac.il (Assaf Razon)
(smirk)

A few of my friends and I attended a wedding. Being from Israel, this is
naturally a Jewish wedding, and one of my friend is an ex combat officer
in the Army.

So we were talking about throwing the bouquette, and I said (being the
insistant wedding-basher) that if one were thrown at me, I'd dodge it.
So my friend said: "I'll just throw it at you like the Palestinians throw
rocks and bombs at us", and I answered "you mean a 'Mazeltov Cocktail'?"
