Lottery Mega-Inflation

david@infopro.netcom.com (Dragon [David Fiedler])
"InfoPro Systems: Writers, Consultants, and Dragons"
(smirk, true)

From "Lucky Times," newsletter of the California Lottery, Vol. 1, No. 1:

First of all, the top prize - $25,000--is the largest Scratcher instant prize the Lottery has offered in nearly two years. While most Scratcher game top prizes range from $500 to $5,000, this one lives up to its name: Megabucks!

No wonder the California budget has problems, if they can't tell the difference between thousands and millions!
