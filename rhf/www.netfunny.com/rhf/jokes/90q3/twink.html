A girl's first haircut

wehr@sharkey.cc.umich.edu (Bruce Wehr)
Ford Motor Company - Electronics Division
(sexual, chuckle)

Soupy Sales used this at a local comedy club (as best as I can remember it):

A young girl is sitting in a barber shop with her mother, eating a twinkie, and anxiously awaiting her first haircut. When her turn comes, she brings her twinkie with her to the chair, and the barber covers her. Soon, she pulls the twinkie out for a bite.

"You're getting hair on your twinkie," the barber playfully warns.

"Yes, I know," replies the girl. "And I'm getting boobs, too."
