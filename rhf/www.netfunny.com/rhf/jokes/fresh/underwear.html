Recently, singer k. d. lang had a chance to sing for the President and
First Lady. Afterwards, President Clinton asked her what she liked most
and least about singing on tour. She said that her favorite part was the
performing, but her least favorite was "when people throw underwear at you
when you're on stage. But that's okay, because you know it's really hard
to do laundry when you're on the road."
