Protester Satisfaction Survey

bscott@nyx.cs.du.edu (Ben Scott)
(smirk, original)

I worked for a while at an office which shall remain nameless, but whose
business does, from time to time, attract protest groups.  I and a 
colleague named Linda wrote this one morning while beseiged by an
especially radical group.  

(Note that I'm as much of an environmentalist as the next guy - I'm
a registered Democrat and never listen to Rush Limbaugh.  But sometimes
you meet people who take even the worthiest causes far too seriously.
I did not do this to offend anyone, merely to break the tension at the
office that morning.  (as if the misspelled protest signs weren't doing
that already...)  Anyway, all disclaimers in effect, and I'm definitely
not targeting anyone or any group in particular.
