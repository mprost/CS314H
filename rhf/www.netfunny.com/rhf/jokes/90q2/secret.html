danzig@eniac.seas.upenn.edu (David Danzig)
(original, funny)

I wrote this and submitted it to my school's humor magazine (Punch Bowl), where it was published. I thought I'd share it with the net. It is a parody of tabloid newspaper advertisements. Enjoy

--David

AMAZING SECRET

Hi! My name is David Danzig! Not long ago, I was penniless! I had no friends! I had nothing to live for! I had climbed to the roof of a very tall building in scenic downtown Philadelphia, and was about to do myself in, when I discovered the AMAZING SECRET that CHANGED MY LIFE!!

MONEY! FAME! HAPPINESS!!!!!

Today, I am one of the world's richest men! I am internationally celebrated as one of the world's most popular human beings! I live an exciting, fulfilling life, AND ALL BECAUSE OF MY AMAZING SECRET!!!!!!!!!!

WHAT IS THIS SECRET?

Just what is this amazing secret? I swore to myself that I would never reveal the source of my INCREDIBLE SECRET, but I can share the secret with you. Since I am so FANTASTICALLY rich, I can give you my WONDERFUL secret ABSOLUTELY FREE!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

WHY?

Why would I make this offer? When you see how REMARKABLE and AMAZING this FREE secret is, I am confident that you will feel indebted to me, and volunteer gifts! If not, then keep the secret with no obligation! It's that easy!!! You have NOTHING to lose!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
