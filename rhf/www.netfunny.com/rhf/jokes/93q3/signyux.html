Just a few interesting signs (and my attempting-to-be-humorous commentary):

"Paul's Collision Experts"
   (don't people have enough accidents without needing a specialist?)

"National State Bank"
   (well, which is it?)

Movie theater: "'Toys' now showing on two screens"
   (why... can't it fit on one?)

Restaurant: "Breakfast & Sandwich Exchange"
   (do you know where your food has been?)

Seen in a bar at a deaf university: "Last call 1AM"
   (yeah, but who will hear it?)
