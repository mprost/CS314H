Just Sit Back and Relax

arcie@netcom.com (Randy Cassingham)
(chuckle)

The American Correctional Association recently held their
convention.  The AP did a story on the event (which ran on wires
on Aug 13), and part of it focussed on the vendors selling goods
to help deal with the ever-increasing tide of prisoners.  This
classic line from the story needs wider distribution:

     "Business is good," said salesman Ron Smith, who was
     selling a sleek black chair meant for restraining the
     truly unruly, a cross between a straitjacket on wheels
     and a sadomasochist's Barca-lounger.

What a picture THAT paints!
