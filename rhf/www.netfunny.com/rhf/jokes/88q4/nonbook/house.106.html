I've found a sure-fire way to give burglars the impression that I'm
home - I leave my second car idling in the driveway while I'm gone.
It only works for trips of four days or less, or else the car runs
out of gas.  During the summer months I sometimes put some groceries in
the trunk and leave the lid open, giving the impression that "I just
ran into the house." :-)
