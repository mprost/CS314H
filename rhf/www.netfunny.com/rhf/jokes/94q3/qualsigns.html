Quality processes

fadden@uts.amdahl.com (Andy McFadden)
(smirk, office)

We've had a number of changes around here at Amdahl, including a lot of
people moving into our building from other parts of the company.  Somebody
brought a few "quality reminder" signs with them, and put them around
the building.

Before too long, they started showing up in strange places (on desks,
upside-down against doors, etc).  My favorite is a sign that says:

	Quality is an attitude

	Let's all check quality,
	Our customers do!

It's in the men's room.
