Sometimes an article is posted in such a style as to be difficult to understand. While we all know there is one and only one correct understanding (mine), and no "interpetation" is involved, sometimes is can be helpful to the novice if we give some hints. Recently an article was posted to comp.sys.ibm.pc.hardware.chips, comp.sys.intel and other places, which might have seemed off topic, but this is merely due to the lack of correct understanding among non-believers. Herewith you will find an annotated version of the message in question, which makes more clear its relevance to the group.


> The earthquake in Los Angeles, California, the flood in Europe, the
> seemingly unstoppable war in the former Yugoslavia, the devastating
> fires in Australia, the flood in the Midwest of the United States of
> America, the devastating fires near Los Angeles, California, the rapid
> and appalling increase in violence in cities, towns, villages all
> over the world, the famines, the diseases, the rapid decline of the
> family unit, and the destructive earthquake in India (in 1993) are
> signs that this world's history is coming to a climax.

Recently there have been reports of vibrations, unstable power supplies, saturated bandwidths, irresolvable buss conflicts, chip overheating, increasing voltage spikes on the signal lines, and lack of proper architectural continuity between processor generations.


> The human race has trampled on God's Constitution,

It's all the fault of RISC (Reckless Involvement with Satanic Competitors). Constant Indoctrination in Suitable Concepts is the only solution.


> God's Holy Spirit is gradually withdrawing its protection from the
> earth and the devastating events you see are demonstrations of Satan's
> power. All those who are not guarded by God are in danger of forever
> losing eternal life.

If it doesn't say "Intel Inside" you could be in deep trouble; and if we continue to lose market share your products might be rapidly obsoleted.


> If you want to know what's about to happen, please study the books of
> Daniel and Revelation which are located in God's Word, the Bible. They
> are not sealed or closed books. They can and must be understood by all.

This is an Open Standard, and compliance is manditory.


> Every word in the Bible from Genesis to Revelation is true. The Bible and
> the Bible only must be your guide.

The authors assume no liability for typographical or other errors in this manuscript. Failure to follow the instructions exactly will void your warranty. Do not acquire literature from any other source [see note at end, though], we are "soul sourcing" this one and will persecute infringers.


> When God's Law (the Constitution for the Universe) is consistently
> ignored, disregarded, changed, and questioned, He permits certain events
> to occur to wake us up.

Our low power units exit from sleep mode with an interrupt when any of a variety of serious errors occur.


> I would urge all, wherever you are and regardless of the circumstances,
> to directly call on Jesus and ask Him to intervene in your life.

Our marketing department's 800 number will follow this article - they can tell you why you must use our chips. (Those who use other chips will go into our legal department's database for later punitive actions).


> For reference I'm including God's Constitution as given in the King James
> Version Bible.

An excerpt from the FAQ (version KJV) follows.


> Please note that when God says the seventh day, he means Sabbath
> (the 7th day of the week) not Sunday (1st day of the week).

All bit and buss cycle numbers in our reference manuals are actually 1 based rather than 0 based as most users have misconceived them. Those using 0 based addressing or timing will suffer from the afflicitions mentioned above. Note that untold millions who thought they were true believers have been caught by this slip-up and will reap eternal perdition as a result, for breaking a Commandment. Read the fine print! (PS: This point is not actually in the document KJV, which is to be your only guide, but I've told you it's true and you will be held accountable!)

> Commandment #1: Exodus 20:1-3 (KJV) And God

> the LORD thy God, which have brought
> thee out of the land of Egypt, out

> for the LORD will not hold him

Our lawyers are gonna roast anyone who falsely labels anything "Intel Inside" (tm).
