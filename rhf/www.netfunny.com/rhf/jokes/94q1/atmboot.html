Teaching your Automatic teller who's boss...

simon@internode.com.au (Simon Hackett)
(smirk, computers, original)

I read a magazine yesterday in which Digital were showing off shiny
pictures of the inside of some new ATM (Automatic Teller Machines, not
that strange new term for X.25 on steroids) machines being installed
in Australia.

It was rather un-inspiring to see that our financial liveihood is now
being entrusted to Digital 386SX/25 PC's, lurking in the bottom of an
otherwise fairly empty ATM cabinet...

I wonder what happens if you hold down "Withdraw", "Cancel" and "OK"
all at once?

Simon Hackett & Adrian Corston
service@internode.com.au
