 This came from a satirical Norwegian TV show, called "Egentlig" ("Really") on
 NRK 1. It was broadcast on Friday Feb 16th.

 It is a well-known fact that much illegal, pornographic and dangerous material
 is being distributed via the Internet, and that various groups are fighting to
 rid the world of this. Now, rumor has it that many of these groups are also
 planning a campaign against a well-known way of distributing said material, by
 completely banning the use of paper. "It's everywhere", says a young so-called
 papernerd. "You just can't stop it, even if some people use it to distribute
 illegal material." Says a Christian People's Party representative, "The only
 way to stop this problem from destroying our society is to take away the means
 by which it is done."

 Sic transit gloria mundi...
