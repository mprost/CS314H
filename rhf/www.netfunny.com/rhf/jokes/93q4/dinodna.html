Really old bugs

tolkin@ksr.com (Steve Tolkin)
(maybe, computers)

A programmer tracking a bug in the Unix kernel finally exclaims: 
"I can't believe how old this bug must be!"

His office mate replies: 
"Is it old enough to have dinosaur DNA in it?"
