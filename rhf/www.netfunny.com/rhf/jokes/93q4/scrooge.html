Psychiatrist's "Bah, humbug!"

brahm@fermi.phys.cmu.edu (David Brahm)
(topical, smirk, xmas)

The Pittsburgh Post-Gazette (12/19/93) quotes Dr. Karl Menninger, American
psychiatrist (1893-1990), on the transience of the Christmas spirit:

"I never could see why people were so happy about Dickens' `A Christmas
Carol' because I never had any confidence that Scrooge was going to be
any different the next day."
