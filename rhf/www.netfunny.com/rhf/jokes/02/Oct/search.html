Searching for an item at Amazon

rhf@gordol.org (Jeffrey Kaplan)
OCG Agamemnon
(smirk, computers)

My sister is a historical reenactor, and is looking for a specific Italian book. So I did a search at amazon.com for it. Minus the magazine ads, here's the result from my search:


>---------------------------------<
Books Search Results: we were unable to find exact matches for your
search for 'Velluti e Moda tra XV e XVII secolo'.

Close matches for this search:
The Lost King of France: A True Story of Revolution, Revenge, and DNA

Letters to Penthouse XVII

Secretos de Una Experta Del Mundo de la Moda
>---------------------------------<

Gotta love those implicit "or" searches...
