A powerful report

murphycm@helios.aston.ac.uk (Chris Murphy)
(smirk, government)

A friend of mine just mentioned that he's off to visit a nuclear power
station as part of his studies.  "It's just been re-commissioned" he
told us, "and it's had a major re-fit."

We couldn't help but laugh when he went on to tell us about the
"glowing report" the authorities gave it.
