Califonia -- The Environmental State??

jagherlo@nps.navy.mil (Joseph A. Gherlone Jr)
(smirk, awww, true)

  This really happened, and I believe falls into the "...from the mouths of
babes ..." category.

  While explaining the word "environment" to my three-year-old daughter, I 
used the word in several contexts, ending with the generic usage of "nature."

At this point I said, "Nature is the environment we all live in."
She replied, "Daddy, we don't live in an environment."
"No??" said I.
"No," she said with confidence, "We live in California."
