How do you spell that?

a207@lehigh.edu (Thomas F. Zadlo)
(smirk, guys)

There were two men sitting in a hunting club.  One says to the other
"I think it's spelled W-H-H-O-O-O-M-B."

The other man replies
"No, wouldn't it be more like W-H-H-H-O-O-M-M-B-B?"

The waitress is walking by and says
"You guys are both idiots!  It's spelled W-O-M-B you jerks!"
and she storms off.  The one man turns to his friend and says
"How do you like that?  She's heard an elephant fart too!"
