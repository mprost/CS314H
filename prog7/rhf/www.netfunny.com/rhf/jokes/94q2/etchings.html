New life in an old cliche

JRP1@phx.cam.ac.uk (Jonathan R. Partington)
(original, smirk, sexual, computers)

 We've had quite a lot of computer equipment stolen lately, so
what's left has now all been etched with the legend "Leeds Univ.
Maths" using a fancy electric engraving machine. Before I did
mine, the secretary led me into her office to show me what the
finished artwork should look like.

 So for the first time in my life someone has invited me in to
see their etchings.
