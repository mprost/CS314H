IBM VM Joke

SCOTH@wmvm1.cc.wm.edu ( Scott Hammer)
(computer, chuckle)

Just a bit of pre-Memorial Day IBM humor.  About 1978, IBM put out a poster
touting the virtues of VM.  On this poster was the text:

If it's there and you can see it -- it's real
If it's not there and you can see it -- it's virtual
If it's there and you can't see it -- it's transparent
If it's not there and you can't see it -- you erased it!

Sorry if this is too much of an old chestnut for some of you -- this is the
first I'd heard of it.
--
-Scott Hammer
 College of William and Mary
