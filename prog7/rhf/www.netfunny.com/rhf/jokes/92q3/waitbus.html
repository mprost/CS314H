Closet

cerrlss@techunix.technion.ac.il (Shahar Steiff)
(chuckle, sexual)

This lady calls the carpenter and complains that her closet shakes
every time the bus stops at the bus-stop next to her house. 
When the carpenter comes, the lady shows him to the closet, when suddenly
her husband arrives home. Opening the closet he notices a stranger
sitting inside it and asks him : "What the &%#$ are YOU doing here ???"
so the carpenter replies : "If I told you I was waiting for the bus,
would you believe me ?"
