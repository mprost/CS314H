How to have the first OJ book after the verdict is out...  


OJ.h

#define OUTCOME  NOT PROVEN 
#define GREEDIEST_JUROR  XXX
#define TRIAL_LENGTH 8
#define LARGE_TIME_UNIT months
#define MEDIUM_TIME_UNIT weeks
#define SMALL_TIME_UNIT days
\...


   $OUTCOME!: The True Story of the Trial of the Century
           by $GREEDIEST_JUROR

The trial had gone on for $TRIAL_LENGTH $LARGE_TIME_UNIT. $SMALL_TIME_UNIT 
had turned into $MEDIUM_TIME_UNIT$ and $MEDIUM_TIME_UNITS into 
$LARGE_TIME_UNIT.  $NUMBER_WITNESS witnesses had been paraded in 
front of us including the dubious $BIG_CLOWN and the notorious $BIG_IDIOT,
as well as spot-light snatching $LESSER_CLOWN and unbelievable $LESSER_IDIOT.
The prosecution had spent an incredible $ $PROSECUTE_MONEY, though this 
was dwarfed by the $ $DEFENSE_MONEY spent by the defense.  

In the end, we knew that the only possible verdict was $OUTCOME ...
