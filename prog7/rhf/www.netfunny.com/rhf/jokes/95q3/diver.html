This is absolutely true.... I'm not sure it's that funny, but
being a diver, I thought it was funny.

Apparently, there was a fire on the island of Paros (sp?) in
Greece. Since the authorities don't have firehydrants like you
would find in the US, they use a helicopter-based system to scoop
water from the ocean and dump it on the fire. Well, unfortunately, the
mananged to scoop up a scuba diver in the bucket. No, he didn't live
(he died in the fall, not from the fire).

I guess this gives new meaning to the idea of being prudent what you
take from the sea. 

Yes, it's sick, sick, sick... enjoy.
