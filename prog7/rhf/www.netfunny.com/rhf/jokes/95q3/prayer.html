Saying Grace

roc@atl.hp.com (Bob Calvert)
Hewlett-Packard Company, NAFO Technology Solutions Lab
(smirk)

My brother plays in a Christian band and the bass player related this story
about his son, Josh.

4 year old Josh and his family were visiting grandparents for a meal, and
Grandpa asked Josh if he wanted to say the blessing. So Josh, and everyone else
bowed their heads, and Josh started mumbling the blessing at such a low volume
that Grandpa couldn't make out what he was saying.

When grace was over (with a louder AMEN), Grandpa says, "Josh, it was very nice 
of you to say the blessing for us, but I couldn't hear what you said."

Josh replied, "I wasn't talking to YOU!".
