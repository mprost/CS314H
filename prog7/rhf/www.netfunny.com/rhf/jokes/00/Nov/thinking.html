Always thinking...

ether@cirl.uoregon.edu (David W. Etherington)
(original, smirk)

My six-year-old is always thinking. Yesterday a fireman came to his school to talk to the kids about fire safety. As Ian was sitting in the tub going over his day with me, he told me about it.

"The fireman said, 'If you hear a sound like <insert 6-year-old's rendition of fireman's rendition of the gurgling sound of a respirator>, you shouldn't run and hide, because it's a good thing. It means that there is a fireman coming to rescue you.' But I was thinking in my head that maybe it's a bad thing, because it means your house is on fire."

He's got a point.
