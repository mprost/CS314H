MAKE MONEY FAST!!!

Here's a way to make more money than you ever dreamed possible, PLUS you
get to help build the Information Superhighway at the same time.

The instructions are simple.

1.  Buy an Internet connection from the provider named at the top of the
    following list.

    a.  PSI
    b.  UUNET
    c.  Alternet
    d.  SURNET
    e.  The World

2.  Remove that provider, moving everyone else on the list up one.

3.  Start a company that provides Internet connections.  (You've got one
    now, so why not?)

4.  Add your company to the bottom of the list.

5.  Send a copy of the new list, along with these instructions, to all
    of your friends.

Within the next few weeks, thousands of people will call you to get
Internet connections.

DON'T BREAK THE CHAIN!!!
