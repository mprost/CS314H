The Fiddler

steves@sparc.SanDiegoCA.NCR.COM (Steve Schlesinger)
(smirk)

My cousin works in one of the major music stores in
midtown Manhattan.  He told this story that actually happened.

Itzhak Perlman, world renowned violinist, is a frequent customer
and is generally well known by store employees.  During a visit
he asked if he could use the restroom.  There is no rest room
for customers on the showroom floor.  A relatively new employee
who had been helping Perlman was asked to escort him in the
back elevator to the rest room on the 3rd floor.

The employee says to Perlman, "You're a pretty good fiddler.
Do you play with anyone special?"

Without missing a beat, Perlman replies:
	"If someone calls me, I play."
