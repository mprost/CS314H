My sig. reveals all ...

jha@manx2.demon.co.uk (John Atkinson)
(original, smirk)

Do not mess with DNA sequencers.

A well-known worker in the Human Genome Project
whose major interest is sequencing DNA is sending
out email with a highly original sig.

His email takes a whole week to download.

His sig. is 3 billion lines long.

--
John Atkinson
