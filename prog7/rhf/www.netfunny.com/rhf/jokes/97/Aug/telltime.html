Time and math

neufeld@onyx.southwind.net (Gwen Neufeld)
(smirk)

We occasionally have training sessions and send the announcement to our
staff via email for the correct time of 6:30pm. Apparently, the guy in
charge was a bit preoccupied leading to several corrections as follows:

*First email*
	To whoever this concerns (you know who you are)

	SouthWind University will be held
	Thu Jul 10 12:30:00 CDT 1997

	Be there.
	Arnold Cavazos, Jr.                   

*The second followed almost immediately*

	To whoever this concerns (you know who you are)

	SouthWind University will be held
	Thu Jul 10 16:30:00 CDT 1997
                    ^
	Be there.
	Arnold Cavazos, Jr.                

	SouthWind University will be held

	On Thursday at 6:30 pm

	Be there.
	Arnold Cavazos, Jr.                

*This came as a followup from a sysadmin*

	>> SouthWind University will be held
	>>
	>> On Thursday at 6:30 pm
	>>
	>> Be there.

	The first class will be on the subject of telling time...

	(:-)
