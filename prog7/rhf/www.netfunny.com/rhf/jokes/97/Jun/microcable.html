Microsoft Cable Network

Basscraft@aol.com
(smirk, computers)

Multiple choice:

Microsoft is negotiating a deal with a major cable television company. This
will result in:

(a) Enhanced cable systems which will revolutionize both the cable-TV
industry and the home computer industry with interactive cable and digital
Internet television

OR

(b) Seinfeld reruns that freeze up in the middle of the episode for no
apparent reason, requiring you to shut down and restart the episode, losing
all unsaved jokes

The correct answer is (b).


[Paraphrased from Dave Ross, a radio columnist on the CBS Radio Network
broadcast 6/12/97]
