"Make $$$ Fast" leads to Revolution...

andreas@netcom.com (Andreas Ramos)
Netcom On-Line Services
(smirk, USENET)

It had to happen. Several weeks ago, a pyramid scheme (aka "Make $$$
Fast!: Just add your name to this list and send $10 to the first five
names..." etc.) collasped in southern Albania...

 * which led to tens of thousands of people losing their life savings
 * which led to massive demonstrating
 * which led to storming the police
 * which led to widespread looting and rioting
 * which spread to the capital Tirana
 * which led to the looting of the military academy's weapons supply,
 * which led to evacuation of the US embassy 
 * which led to pleas from the government of Albania for NATO to take over
   the country and restore order. 

All because of an idiotic "Make $$$ Fast!" scheme! 

The next spammer should be shot for insurrection.
