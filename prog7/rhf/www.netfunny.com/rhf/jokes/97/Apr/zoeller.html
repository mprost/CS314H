Fuzzy thinking

cradock@ssc.wisc.edu (Bob Cradock)
(topical, smirk, racial stereotypes, offense=Thai folks)

Golfer Fuzzy Zoeller apologized for suggesting that Tiger Woods
shouldn't request a menu of fried chicken and collard greens at
the Masters tournament banquet.

Zoeller explained, "I'm sorry if I offended Tiger, his father,
or any other black Americans.  What I meant to say was I hope 
he doesn't ask for fried _dog_".

Woods and his mother were unavailable for comment.
