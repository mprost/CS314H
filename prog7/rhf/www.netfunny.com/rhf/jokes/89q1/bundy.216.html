Tasteless Ted Bundy Aeronautical Joke.

witters@tc.fluke.COM (John Witters)
(topical, maybe)

The warden had bad news and good news for Ted Bundy.

The bad news was that he was to be executed in a few hours.

The good news was that Boeing wired the electric chair.

(Explanation: There is speculation that engine instruments were cross wired on the British Midlands 737 that crashed last month in Britain. Boeing has also discovered cross wired cargo hold fire extinguishers on some 757s and 767s. I assume everyone knows Ted Bundy was a mass murderer recently executed in Florida.)
