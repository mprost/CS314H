Q: How many greenpeace researchers does it take to change a light bulb?
A: Two, one to put in the new one and one to recycle the old one.

Q: How many soviet politburo members does it take to change a light bulb?
A: None, the light bulb is just sick with a bad cold.

Q: How many Frank Zappa's does it take to change a light bulb?
A: None, the light bulb is not dead, it just smells funny.

Q: How many system administrators does it take to change a light bulb?
A: None, just remove the rights of everybody allowed to go into the room.
