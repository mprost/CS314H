Working at 7-11

(These jokes attributable to Michael Floorwax, a Denver comedian
 who plays comedy clubs around the country, and is also a morning

Do you ever wonder why 7-11 stores insult their employees?  Right
there on the storefront window, it says "Clerk has no money".  Of
course s/he has no money, s/he gets paid $4 an hour.  Geez, they
already have a low-life job, why is it necessary to insult them
more?
