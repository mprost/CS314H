Intel vs. AMD

brian@engadm3.csd.mot.com (Brian Smithson)
Motorola Inc., Computer Group, Computer Systems Division
(topical, computer, smirk)

You may have read that Intel recently filed suit with AMD.  It seems that
both companies employ someone named Mike Webb, and while both Webbs happened
to be staying at the same hotel, some AMD announcement materials about the
Am386 were mistakenly sent to Intel's Webb.  Intel figured that this infringed
on their trademark of the name "386", and filed suit accordingly.

Clearly, AMD is missing an opportunity if they don't file a countersuit with
Intel over Intel's use of the name "Mike Webb".
