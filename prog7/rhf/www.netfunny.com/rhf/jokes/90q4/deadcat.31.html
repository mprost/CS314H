This is from our university newpaper, The Western Front COPS BOX, 3-6-90Ths

-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=
	A woman called police after discovering a dead cat in her front
yard in the 2400 block of Ellis Street.  The woman also reported her dog
had been sick for a couple of days and suspected the dog may have consumed
a poisonous substance, similar to that which she suspects led the cat to 
its terrible fate.
	The officers put the cat in a sack for the woman, so she could take
it in for a autopsy.  The dog, however, recovered from his illness and is
doing fine, but the cat is still dead and results of its autopsy have not
yet been reported to the police.
-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=

Well, I wonder if the cat is alive today?
