Bush and Hussein on TV

allans@ebay.sun.com (Allan Meers "Just say NO to hackers!")
(chuckle)

President Bush was seen on Iraqi TV for a 10 minute speech,
explaining to Iraq that they can't win.  His speech was
then analyzed, re-explained, re-interpreted, and then
denounced for various reasons.

Saddam Hussein has asked for equal time on American TV,
but the networks won't commit to showing his tape until 
they have seen it, and decided whether or not it had
any audience appeal.  He may be the first leader of a country
that is kicked out because of bad ratings.


This is the next Phase of Reagan's  "Star Wars"  plan.
