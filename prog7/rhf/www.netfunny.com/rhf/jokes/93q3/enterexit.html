Where's the "exit" key?

volpe@ausable.crd.ge.com (Christopher R Volpe)
(smirk, computers)

The electronic timecard recording program at our site uses an employee's
electronic "signature" (essentially a password) that must be changed
periodically. When the user changes his/her signature, the system
displays the following message:

         SIGNATURE SUCCESSFULLY MODIFIED.                                     
         PRESS 'ENTER' TO EXIT PROGRAM....                                    

I half-expect to walk to the elevators at the end of the day and see a 
sign that says "Press 'UP' to go down".
