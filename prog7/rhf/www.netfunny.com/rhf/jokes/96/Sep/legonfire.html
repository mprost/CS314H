Fuel for the fire

Rob.dean/ou=om1/ou=hp-pinewood@pwd.hp.com
(chuckle, sick)

Over lunch today (18th Sep 96) with a number of colleagues (original as
far as I know, final comment from Emma Sheard):

Discussion over lunch turned to consideration of alternative forms of
energy. After covering some of the more mundane options (wind, sea
etc.), an amputation hospital in the north of England was mentioned.

The hospital is heated from a furnace, which, amongst other things, is
used to incinerate the amputated limbs and other body parts.

The response to this fascinating fact, "So when it gets cold, you just
throw another leg on the fire?"
