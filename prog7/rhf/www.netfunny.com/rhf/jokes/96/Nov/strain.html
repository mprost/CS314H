Dad's Humor

ronald@hpvclrl.vcd.hp.com (Ron Paul)
(smirk, heard it)

This true tid-bit I attribute to my dear father, Ben, known for his dead-pan 
dry humor.

As a young boy, I was rocking rather enthusiastically on our great-big old
rocking chair in our living room ajacent to our screened back door.

Walking through, my dad stopped briefly and dryly admonished, " Don't rock
too hard. You'll fall through the screen door and strain yourself." And 
promptly went along his way.

Took me a while thereafter ( a couple of years ) to get it.
