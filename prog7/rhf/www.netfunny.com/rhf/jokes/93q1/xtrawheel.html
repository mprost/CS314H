Extra wheel

sagar@ece.concordia.ca
(smirk, computers)

One day I was in a very-low priced PC store looking for a suitable
machine. Another man was also there in the store talking with
the store owner as follows:

Man: I want to have the system an extra meg. of memory.
Owner: No problem. That will cost you only $75.

Man: But, how and when can I use the extra meg?
Owner: You can use it whenever your programs need it.

Man: Then what is its use if I can't use it all the time?
Owner: (After a pause) You know, it is just like the extra wheel in your car.
