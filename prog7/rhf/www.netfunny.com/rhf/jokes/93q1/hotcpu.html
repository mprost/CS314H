Which is Hotter?

ntmtv!robin@ames.arc.nasa.gov (Robin Coutellier)
(smirk, sexual, original)

After 6 years together, both as a couple and working in the same company,
my boyfriend and I split about 3 months ago.  As luck would have it, soon 
after we broke up I had to start working on a project in development 
that required me to beep him whenever I needed assistance with it.  Last 
week I was having a terrible time getting the machine to boot and stay 
booted and had to keep beeping him.  He determined that the CPU board was 
too hot.  His temporary solution was:

        1.  Take it out
        2.  Wave it around a lot
        3.  Shove it back in

I think he misses me ...
