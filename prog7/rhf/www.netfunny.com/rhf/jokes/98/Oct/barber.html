Clinton-free foam

Hugh.David@eurocontrol.fr (Hugh David)
EUROCONTROL Experimental Centre
(original, smirk, sexual)

Cans of shaving foam sold in the UK are also sold in Scandinavia, and labelled accordingly.

It seems that the Swedish for 'Shaving Foam' is BARBERSKUM.

Depending on where you place the S this is either disappointing or absolutely revolting.
