Sincerely (Really!)

herbw@wiskit.rain.com (Herb Weiner)
Kitchen Wisdom Publishing Company, Portland, Oregon USA
(smirk)

The following was found in a box of computer paper in the mid 70's.
It is printed on pastel pink paper, approximately 3.5 x 6 inches,
with pin-feed holes on both sides.  It is neatly printed in black
ink, except for the company name, which is blue, and the signature,
which appears to be from a rubber stamp:

MOORE BUSINESS FORMS, INC.
HONESDALE PENNA.

We have taken all precautions to print, assemble and
package your business forms to your specifications.

Each of us, who worked on your order, is aware that
your business forms are our business and your satisfaction
is our guarantee of continued success.

We aim to serve you continuously with a quality product.

                     Sincerely, 2043
