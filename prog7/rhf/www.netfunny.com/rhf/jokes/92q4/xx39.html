	In the magazine New Scientist, there is an article about the
U.S. Patent and Trademark office disallowing the logo for the 
red, white, and blue "Old Glory" condoms.  The logo, with the Stars 'n
Stripes portrayed as a condom-shaped banner, was deemed "scandalous."

	The headline on the article was:

	"Men banned from raising the flag"
