Easily offended old ladies


U of Rochester, CS Dept, Rochester, NY
(heard it, chuckle)

(This joke was stolen from Paul Theroux's Picture Palace and Theroux probably got it from somewhere else before that.)

Old lady calls up police department: Officer, there is a man exposing himself in the next building.

Dispatcher: We'll be right over, lady.

(Five minutes later at her apartment.)

Officer: Which way, lady?

OL: This way officer, he's still shamelessly baring himself.

Officer: Where is he, lady? I don't see no naked man.

OL: Oh, you have to look through this telescope.


Ken
