Wheel of fortune

mfrancisco@ucsd.edu (SweetPinkPig)
(heard it, smirk, science)


This is a joke that my Organic Chemistry Lab professor showed to me in
office hours the other day.  He said he got it from his Chemistry
professor when he was a freshman studying at USC some time ago.  Here is
the structure.  Please guess what you think it is:

  		Fe_____Fe
  	        /      \
  	      Fe        Fe
  	       |        |
              Fe        Fe
              	\      /
  		Fe----Fe


It's a heme, no, no, it's a stop sign made of iron, NO, NO!

It's a "FERROUS WHEEL"

--
Lyn (smiling at you from UC San Diego :-)
