Charley (the hurricane) MUST be a Floridian...

cupasoup@pele.cx (Jack C. Lipton)
(originalsmirk)

I noticed watching Charley go by on the TV news (I live in the Tampa Bay region) that there was a lot of lightning in the NorthEast quadrant of the storm, that Charley drove like a stereotypical Floridian.

After all, he made an unexpected hard right turn and then kept on signaling for a right turn as he drove on up to Daytona.
