Church Bulletin Blooper

rolfet@earthlink.net (Tim Rolfe)
(smirk)

The bulletin for the Cathedral of Our Lady of Lourdes in Spokane for June 5-6 has a recommendation for a publication that gives the assigned readings for the Sundays of the year.

Part of the text tells of how the book has been helpful for a number of "shut-ins" --- except that the typist unfortunately hit the key to the RIGHT of "u" on the QWERTY keyboard --- "i." Strangely, this wasn't caught by a spell-checker.
