Fashion Cafe's Gimmick

Unknown
(smirk, offense=people with eating disorders)

With the much hyped opening of the "Fashion Cafe," (the restauraunt
enterprise of several supermodels), it's clear there are only two things 
to do when one's career is fading -- release an album (Milla, Hulk 
Hogan[!]), or open a restaurant (see: Planet Hollywood).

Still, Two questions remain.

First, what do supermodels know about _food_?

Secondly, what gimmick there could be left for the venture?  "Planet
Hollywood" has DeLoreans, "Hard Rock Cafe" has car parts embedded in the
wall...

I've got the answer:  The toilets in the lavatories placed at the more
convenient height of four-and-a-half feet. 

"Finally, no knee-bruises from cold tile floors -- and less splatter too!"
