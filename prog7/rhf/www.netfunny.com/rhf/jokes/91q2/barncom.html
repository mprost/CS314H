Burger King's ad slogan

max@darkside.com (Max Pandaemonium)
The Dark Side of the Moon +1 408 245 SPAM
(original, true, chuckle)

My girlfriend and some of her friends went in to Burger King a few months ago, interested in getting those "Barnyard Commandos" that they were offering a while ago. She walked up to the counter, asked for them, and was politely informed that one has to purchase something to get them.

She then said, "Sometimes you gotta break the rules."

She got them.
