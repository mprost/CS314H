OKC Tornados

dyniece@webzone.net (Dy Ziegler)
(smirk, sexual, true)

In the wake of the May 3 tornados that ravaged Oklahoma City and neighboring areas, many homeowners have tried to deal with the devastation with humor, putting up signs with "For Sale: Fixer-upper" and "Open House" on their now-flattened property.

However, the best one comes from a man in Moore who, before President Clinton's visit, put a sign in his yard saying:


"HEY BILL, HOW'S THIS FOR A BLOW JOB?"
