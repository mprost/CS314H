Marilyn's TV

wumba_man@yahoo.ca (Wumba Man)
(smirk, true)

From the Toronto Sun, regarding the purchase of Marilyn Monroes' TV set. "Rogers" is the Toronto cable company.


>If you think your TV set was expensive, check
>this out: CITY-TV Svengali Moses Znaimer
>recently paid roughly $40,000 Cdn. for this old
>black-&-white TV set. Why? Because it once
>belonged to sex goddess Marilyn Monroe.
>
>Znaimer bought the portable Magnavox last
>October at the record-setting Marilyn Monroe
>Collectibles Auction at Christie's in New York.
>Monroe died in 1962, presumably still waiting for
>Rogers to install her cable.
