Next stop, Ludicrous Speed : Part 2

snowhare@nihongo.org (Benjamin Franz)
(smirk, true)

Last week a national ISP had the mis-fortune to have their banner ad touting "The only thing slowing us down is the speed of light." be juxtaposed with a New York Times article titled ""In a Major Breakthrough, Danish Physicist Slows the Speed of Light" which reported on a scientist who had succeeded in slowing light to a paltry 38 miles per hour.

They now have a new banner ad out (this is for real): 

"The only thing slowing us down.... 
          ...is the occasional group of physicists." 


Touche!
