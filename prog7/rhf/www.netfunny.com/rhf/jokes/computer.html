Computer & Science Jokes

Computer & Science Jokes

This chapter contains jokes about computers, math, engineering and science. Since TeleJoke/RHF is a network of computer users, it's not surprising that there's a slant on computers there. In fact, you'll see jokes here that you just won't see elsewhere because they're for too specialized an audience.

If you're not a computer user--beware. Some of these jokes aren't going to make any sense.

Of course, they may not make sense to computer people either, but that's just part of the conspiracy.
