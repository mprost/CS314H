A mute in a coffee shop

tran@peora.UUCP
Concurrent Computer Corporation, Orlando, Fl
(rec_humor_cull, smirk)

Two guys in a coffee shop. The first guy said to the second, "Do you see that mute over there? I wonder how he orders coffee?" The waitress just passing by, says, "Oh, if you want to know, ordering coffee is easy for him. You have to see him ordering coffee with milk." She blushed and continued, "I have to give him a few slaps before he remove his hands."


Nhan Huu Tran
