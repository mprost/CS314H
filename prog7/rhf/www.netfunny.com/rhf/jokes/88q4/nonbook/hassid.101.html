Orthodoxy in Alabama

roy@phri.UUCP (Roy Smith)
(anti-racist, chuckle)

This Chasidic Jew from New York decides to try his luck in Birmingham, Alabama. When he gets off the bus in Birmingham he notices that all the kids are staring at him. Not being used to being stared at, he find this a bit un-nerving so he turns to the kids and says, "Watsa mattah? You nevah saw a Yhankee before?"
