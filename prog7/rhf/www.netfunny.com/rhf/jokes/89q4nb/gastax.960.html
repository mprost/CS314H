Tax hikes in MA

loverso@xylogics.com (John Robert LoVerso)
Xylogics, Inc., Burlington MA
(chuckle, original)

The news last night of impending tax hikes gave me an inspiration. They plan on raising the gas tax (per gallon) from 5 cents to 11 cents (on Jan 1 or Mar 1), and then to 21 cents on Jan 1 1991. We could have a tax revolt; have everyone dress up as Exxon employees and dump gas into Boston Harbor. Unfortunately, with the way the Harbor is, no one would notice.
