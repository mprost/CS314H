Rest of RHF

This page contains links to pages of links with the rest of the jokes from the early years of rec.humor.funny. These jokes didn't make it into the top 25%, or appeared after 1991 and thus were not culled, so it's not intended that you will want to read them all from this index. Rather, this index is here so net search engines can index these joke to help you find them. Visit the Home Page for more jokes, or the Best of RHF topic index page.

Be warned that this is not a joke collection for children. If you are under 18, check with your parents before browsing these jokes.
