Napster Memorial Page ::

Here Lies Napster
May 1999 - February 2001

Join us, brethren, in the remembrance of the tragic
death of Napster on February 12, 2001.

"Napster was a true American hero. The rest of the world, beyond our borders... you know, countries like Mexico and Africa... we're happy with it, too."
   — George W.

"The aura of Napster was one of beauty and goodness within the human spiritual community."
   — Shirley MacLean

"And if I have prophetic powers, and understand all mysteries and all knowledge, and if I have all faith, so as to remove mountains, but do not have Napster, I am nothing."
   — St. Paul

"Hare Krsna, Hare Krsna, Krsna Krsna, Hare Hare
Hare Napster, Hare Napster, Napster Napster, Hare Hare"
   — The Guy with the Orange Skirt (dancing with tambourine)

"Napster showed me more respect than anyone ever has before."
   — Rodney Dangerfield

"More respect, more respect."
   — Rodney Dangerfield's Backup Singers

"Ding Dong, Napster's Dead, the copyright-infringing evil force is dead..."
   — Dorothy
